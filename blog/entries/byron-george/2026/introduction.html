This site will be maintainable, simple, and intutive. We live in America, where most people believe that the best way to get healthier is to eat a pill ... well this is ringing the opposite approach to this site. Maybe we did peak at HTML. The Internet is about propogating information quickly, and today my timecard took 10 seconds to load. Ask yourself ... to send 2816 bits (14*64 bits for each day's entry, 14*32 bits for each date, 128 bits for the users id, 32*8 bits for jwt auth, 144 bits for the overhead of an HTTP/1.1 request, plus 1000 more bits for things like uri, headers, etc) accross a cable where information travels at the speed of light ... why did it take 10 seconds? And why did I have to see an interactive loading screen while I am waiting? Make your app so fast you don't need a loading screen.
